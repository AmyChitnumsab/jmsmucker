Career

I would like to work in a data science role, but also explore other roles related to working with data. Some fields that I am currently interested in other than data science are data analytics, data engineering, and business analytics. I hope to gain more hands-on experience on working with datasets, creating models, and coming up with solutions that suit business needs.
I would like to explore data-related roles outside of the Information Services department as well. Because data science can be applied to many areas, I am interested in learning more about how each area utilizes data differently.
By seeing the various options that are available, I will be able to better decide which roles best suit my skill levels, work style, and personality.

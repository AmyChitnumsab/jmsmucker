Intern Events

J.M. Smucker Events
The Smucker Internship Program organized many events in between work. These events include an innovation center tour, a coffee session with CEO Mark Smucker, and volunteering at the Pollinator Garden.
The IS Intern Task Force also organized additional career sessions and fun events for the IS interns. Some highlights were the escape rooms in Medina and the IS Intern Olympics.
External Events
Greater Cleveland Partnership hosted multiple events as part of the Summer in the Land Series, bringing together interns from various companies in Ohio. Events include a welcome party at Playhouse Square, an Edgewater beach party, and an upcoming closing party at a bar and arcade.
